Estes

Abilities

Moonlight Immersion

(Active)
Estes restores 250 (+30% Total Magic Power) points of HP for the target hero immediately and links himself with the target for 3s, restoring 325 (+30% Total Magic Power) points of HP for the target slowly. Linking with an ally will also improve his own Attack, and increase the energy charging speed of the Code of Moon Elves. (Scales with the number and the attributes of allied heroes.) Being too far from the target will break the link.

Domain of Moon God

(Active)
Estes drops a flood of moonlight upon the designated area, dealing 350 (+70% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemy units within it. Afterwards, it turns to a domain of Moon Goddess. Enemies will be slowed down by 90% when they touch the barrier of the domain area. Lasts 1.5s. (This slowing Effect will disappear slightly with the time.)

Blessings of Moon Goddess


Estes will immediately cast ehanced Moonlight Immersion to all allied heroes nearby. Enhance Moonlight Immersion within 8s. Meanwhile, he will regenerate 1260 (+105% Total Magic Power) points of HP continously.

Angela

Abilities

Puppet-On-A-String

(Active)
Angela releases a puppet string to the chosen target, dealing 300 (+40% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and gradually slowing the target by up to 80%. If the target is still connected to the string after 3s, they will be immobolized for 1.5s and take 450 (+60% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage. Each stack of Lover's Mark increases the Final Damage by 20%.

Heartguard

(Active)
Angela generates a shield for any chosen ally on the map, absorbing their damage taken up to 1200 (+200% Total Magic Power) points. The shield lasts 6s. After a while, Angela will be attached to the ally for 12s. Dujring the Attached State, Angel can use all her skills without costing Maana. Once this skill is used again or the ally is dead, Angela will be withdrawn from the ally.

Love Waves

(Active)
Angela releases Love Energy in the targeted direction, dealing 170 (+50 % Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and adding Lover's Mark to enemies. Each stack of Lover's Mark increases the amount of damage the target takes by 20%, and slows them by 18%. Lasts 3s, and can be stacked up to 5 times. Restores 100 (+60% Total Magic Power) points of HP to allies of allies are hit. Up to 5 charges can be stored (recharge time is affected by CD Reduction).

Nana

Abilities

Magic Boomerang

(Active)
Nana launches a magic boomerang in the designated direction. Each time the boomerange hits an enemy, it will deal 220 (+80% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and slow enemies by 40% for 1.5s. The first enemy takes full damage, the next one takes 20% less damge, up to 60% less damage.

Molina Blitz

(Active)
Nana summons Molina to attack the designated area 3 times. Each attack deals 400 (+180% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemies in the area and slows them by 50% for 2s. If Molina Blitz hits a single enemy continously, that enemy will be stunned for 1s.

Molina Smooch

(Active)
Nana summons Molina to the designated area. After a short delay, Molina locks onto an enemy hero, chases them, and morphs them, dealing 250 (+50% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and slowing them by 50% for 1.5s. Reduces Magic Defense of transformed heroes by 25%.

Carmilla

Abilities

Crimson Flower

(Active)
Carmilla summons 2 Crimson Flowers to spin around herself for 5 seconds. Enemies hit by the Crimson Flowers will receive 100 / 130 / 160 / 190 / 220 / 250 (+50% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and be slowed down by 30%. Meanwhile, the spinning speed of the Crimson Flowers will be increased for 0.8 second. Each time the Crimson Flowers deal damage to enemies, Carmilla will restore 60 / 78 / 96 / 114 / 132 / 150 (+25% Total Magic Power) HP.

Bloodbath

(Active)
Carmilla accumulates Bloodbath Energy, greatly increasing her Movement Speed by 60%. Use Again: Carmilla casts Bloodbath Energy to a designated enemy, dealing 150 /180 / 210 / 240 / 270 / 300 (+120% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and stunning the enemy for 0.6 second. The damage and the stunning time scale with the Bloodbath Energy accumulation, capped at 100%. The movement speed buff will rapidly decay in 3.5 seconds. Carmilla can only stun enemy heroes or creeps.

Curse of Blood

(Active)
Carmilla casts Curse of Blood in a designated direction. The curse will spread out from the enemy hit to 2 other nearby enemy heroes, dealing 275 / 400 / 525 (+160% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and slowing them down by 90% for 5 seconds. The curse can be inflicted to up to 3 targets at the same time. As long as one target with the Curse of Blood is damaged or controlled, the other influenced targets will also receive damage and crowd control effects

Diggie

Abilities

Auto Alarm Bomb

(Active)
Diggie throws an owl alarm at a designated area and the alarm will remain in a designated location for 30 seconds if there's no enemy units nearby. The alarm will automatically chase any nearby enemy units before exploding and dealing 500 / 560 / 620 / 680 / 740 / 800 (+120% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and slowing them by 30%. If the target gets too far, the alarm bomb will explode prematurely. This skill can be stored up to 3 times. Restore time is affected by cooldown reduction. Alarm Bomb provides vision to its surrounding area as well as bushes when placed inside one.

Reverse Time

(Active)
Diggie locks onto a enemy hero and after a delay or if they enemy gets too far away, Diggie pulls them back to the set location, dealing 150 / 200 / 250 / 300 / 350 / 400 (+100% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage and slowing them by 80%. Reverse Time has a pull distance and will not pull enemies who gets too far away.

Time Journey

(Active)
Upon casting, Diggie removes Crowd Control effects from allies and himself within this skill's range and for 3 seconds, Diggie and his allies will be immune from Crowd Control effects and gain 400 / 550 / 700 (+200% Total Magic Power) points of Shield. If an ally exits this skill's range during the duration of this skill, they will lose all effects provided by this skill, likewise, if an allied hero enters this skill's range during the duration of this skill, they will gain effects provided by this skill
