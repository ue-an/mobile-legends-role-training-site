Khufra

Abilities

Tyrant's Revenge

(Active)
Khufra pulls the bandage on his arms to launch himself in the specified direction, dealing Physical Damage equal to 80 plus 10(+1.5% Total Physical ATK)% of his MAx HP to all enemy units on the path. When blinking to the furthest distance or encountering a first enemy hero, Khufra will immediately stop, dealing Physical Damage equal to 80 plus 10(+1.5% Total Physical ATK) of his Max HP to enemies nearby and then knocking them in the air for up to 1.1s.

Bouncing Ball

(Active)
Khufra uses his bandage to wrap himself into a magic bouncing ball, increasing his own Physical and Magic Defense by 75%. Enemies trying to use blink skills to move across Khufra will be knocked airborne. Each time the magic bouncing ball hits the ground, it will deal Magic Damage equal to 30 plus 6(+2% Total Physical ATK)% of his Max HP to enemies nearby and slow them by 80%. Lasts 0.5s.

Tyrant's Rage

(Active)
Khufra pulls back all enemy targets around him toward the front, dealing 300(+100%Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and slowing them down for 1.25 seconds. If the enemies are knocked against walls, an extra Physical Damage equals to 150% of this skill's damage will be inflicted upon them, and they will be stunned, instead of slowed.

Guardian's Barrier

(Active)
Grock releases a shockwave towards a designated location, dealing 300 (+50% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage. Then the shockwave becomes a stone wall that blockes enemies for 5s.

Wild Charge

(Active)
Grock charges forward, dealing 300 (+50% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage to enemies on the path. Hitting a wall or turret allows him to deal 600 (+150% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage to the nearby enemies and reduce the CD of this skill by 30%.

Abilities

(Active)

(Active)

Meanwhile, Uranus generates an energy shield that absorb up to 300 / 390 / 480 / 570 / 660 / 750 (+200% Total Magic Power) points of damage for 4 seconds. The shield explodes when it is depleted or its duration ends, dealing 300 / 390 / 480 / 570 / 660 / 750 (+200% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to nearby enemies.

Consecration

(Active)
Uranus unleashes energy stored within his body to remove slows on himself and restore 200 / 300 / 400 points of HP immediately, while increasing his Movement Speed by 60% for 8 seconds. (The speed gained decays over time) Uranus immediately acquires 5 / 10 / 15 stacks of Radiance.png, increasing 20% / 25% / 30% of Shield and Regen received within 8 seconds, during which he can't be slowed.
